Privacy Policy for KAIST One Click System

Last updated: January 10, 2025

Introduction

The KAIST One Click System extension automates the two-factor authentication process by interacting with email services like Naver Mail or Gmail. This privacy policy explains how we handle user data and maintain your privacy.

What Data We Collect

The extension does not collect, store, or share any personal data with external servers or third parties. All data processed by the extension remains on the user's local device.

How We Use Permissions

The extension requests permissions such as access to active tabs, scripting, and host permissions solely for the purpose of automating the two-factor authentication process. These permissions are not used for any other purpose.

User Responsibilities

Users are advised to use this extension only on personal and secure devices. Using the extension on shared or public devices may compromise your email account or personal information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date at the top.

Contact Us
